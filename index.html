About Me

Before beginning my journey as a software developer, I’ve worked in customer service positions and in the medical field. I also have been a musician for most of my life. I come from a family of physicians and have come to appreciate and understand what it means to serve people, and make a huge impact on their lives, not just “help” them. People come first in my life, and I believe that value has led me to develop strong interpersonal and communication skills. I naturally gravitate towards opportunities that can challenge me intellectually, allow me to be creative, and give me a chance to invest in any of my passions. After hearing from friends that software development could fulfill all those desires, I knew I had to give it a shot. I’m so happy I took a step towards a career that not only keeps me on my toes every day, but also lets me be part of a team that can only accomplish its goals when its members are truly working together.
